# Purple Merit Technologies: Disputes and Damaged Items
[Source: Internal Policy Pack - Section 5]

1. Damaged/Incorrect Items: If an item arrives damaged or the wrong item is sent, customers must report the issue within 48 hours of delivery.
2. Evidence Required: Photos of the damaged item and the original shipping box are mandatory for all claims.
3. Missing Items: For orders with multiple items, check if items were shipped in separate packages (check tracking). If an item is truly missing from a sealed box, a police report may be required for high-value items ($500+).
4. Marketplace Disputes: For items fulfilled by "Marketplace Sellers," Purple Merit acts as a mediator. We allow 3 business days for the seller to respond before escalating to a "Purple Merit Guarantee" refund.
