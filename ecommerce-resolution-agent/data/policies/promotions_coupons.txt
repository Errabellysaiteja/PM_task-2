# Purple Merit Technologies: Promotions and Coupon Terms
[Source: Internal Policy Pack - Section 4]

1. Usage Limits: Only one coupon code can be applied per order. Coupons cannot be combined with "Flash Sale" pricing.
2. Minimum Purchase: Many coupons require a minimum spend (e.g., "SAVE20" requires a $100+ order).
3. Exclusions: Coupons do not apply to gift cards, shipping fees, or items in the "Electronics" category unless explicitly stated.
4. Expired Coupons: We do not honor expired promotional codes. Support agents are authorized to provide a one-time 5% discount code for frustrated customers but cannot reactivate expired 20%+ codes.
