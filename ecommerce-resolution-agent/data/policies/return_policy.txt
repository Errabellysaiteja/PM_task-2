# Purple Merit Technologies E-Commerce Return & Refund Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

## 1. Standard Return Policy
Customers may return most new, unopened items sold and fulfilled by Purple Merit within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be returned in their original packaging. Return shipping costs will be deducted from the refund unless the return is a result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item).

## 2. Policy Exceptions (Non-Returnable Items)
The following items cannot be returned or refunded under any circumstances:
- Final Sale Items: Any item marked "Final Sale" or purchased with a clearance discount code of 40% or more.
- Hygiene Items: Underwear, swimwear, earrings, and personal grooming products are strictly non-returnable if the hygiene seal is broken.
- Perishables: Food items, flowers, and temperature-sensitive goods cannot be returned. If a perishable item arrives damaged or late, a replacement or refund will be issued upon review of photographic evidence submitted within 24 hours of delivery.
- Opened Software and Video Games: Non-returnable once the factory seal is broken.

## 3. Marketplace Sellers
Items purchased from third-party marketplace sellers on the Purple Merit platform are subject to the individual seller's return policy. Purple Merit's standard 30-day return policy does not automatically apply to marketplace purchases.

## 4. Refund Processing
Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
